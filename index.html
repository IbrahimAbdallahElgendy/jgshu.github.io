Jiangang Shu

Postdoctoral Fellow

City University of Hong Kong

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). My supervisor is Prof. Xiaohua Jia.

I obtained Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong in Feb., 2019., M.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology, and B.Eng. degree in Network Technology and Engineering from Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology in 2015 and 2012, respectively. From Feb. 2018 to Aug. 2018, I was a postgraduate visiting student in Secure Mobile Centre at Singapore Management University, supervised by Prof. Robert H. Deng.

I am passionate about contributing to a further understanding of IOT Security, Crowdsourcing & Cloud Security, Applied Cryptography, Data Security and Privacy, Searchable Encryption, .etc.
