Jiangang Shu

Research Scientist

Peng Cheng Laboratory

I am currently a Research Scientist in Cyberspace Security Research Center, Peng Cheng Laboratory, Shenzhen, China. I obtained Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in Feb., 2019., M.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology, and B.Eng. degree in Network Technology and Engineering from Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology in 2015 and 2012, respectively.

From Feb. 2018 to Aug. 2018, I was a postgraduate visiting student in Secure Mobile Centre at Singapore Management University, supervised by Prof. Robert H. Deng. From Sep. 2018 to Jun. 2019, I was a Research Assistant and Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong , supervised by Prof. Xiaohua Jia.

I am passionate about contributing to a further understanding of IOT Security, Crowdsourcing & Cloud Security, Applied Cryptography, Data Security and Privacy, Searchable Encryption, .etc.
